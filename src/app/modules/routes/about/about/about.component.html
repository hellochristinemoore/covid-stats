About

Motivation for- and state of this website

A collection of "frequently" updated Coronavirus statistics, maps and articles to keep you informed on the local status of the Coronavirus spread. This project started out as an informal playground for me to draw graphs in so that I may better understand the spread of the Coronavirus pandemic we are facing. I published it online realising it might be worthwhile for others to visit/use as well.

There is no funding for this project and is maintained/improved in my spare time. I am not offering this as a service to anyone and I do not purport to have more up-to-to-date or more accurate information than any other statistical websites of this kind. It is very much a "work-in-progress" and might contain erroneous data.

No cookies or personal information of visitors are stored or used in any way.
