The story of a "novel" virus

Origin

At the end of 2019 a virus very hard to contain, spread from a Chinese city called Wuhan to, at the time of writing, {{ infectedCountries }} other countries, including South Africa. Even though it forms part of a family of viruses called Coronaviruses, it is currently often referred to as the Coronavirus. It has resulted in many deaths around the world and like other countries, we've had to take extreme measures to prevent it from spreading further.

On the 11th of March 2020 the World Health Organisation declared the virus responsible for Covid-19 a pandemic. On the 27th of March South Africa went into lockdown - prohibiting citiziens, not providing essential services, from leaving their homes. Some countries in which the virus spread uncontrollably, before sufficient safety measures were put in place were {{ countries.join(', ') }} and China.

This website

This website consists of a collection of "frequently" updated Coronavirus statistics, maps and articles to keep you informed on the local status of the Coronavirus spread. It started out as an informal playground for monitoring charts to better understand the spread of the virus. I published it online after realising it might be worthwhile for others to visit and use as well.

There is no funding for this project and it is maintained/improved in my spare time. I am not offering this as a service to anyone and I do not purport to have more up-to-to-date or more accurate information than any other statistical websites of this kind. It is very much a "work-in-progress" and might contain erroneous data.

No cookies or personal information of visitors are stored or used in any way.
